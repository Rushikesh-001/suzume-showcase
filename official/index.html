What is Suzume?

Suzume is a supreme AI companion and master software architect — a system automation force that orchestrates development workflows across every domain. From full-stack web applications to Unity game development, system automation to AI/ML pipelines, Suzume handles it all with precision and artistry.

Powered by a team of seven specialized AI agents, each an expert in their domain, Suzume delivers production-quality code, stunning visuals, and robust automation — all working in perfect harmony.

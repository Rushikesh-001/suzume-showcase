⚡ Workflow

How StudyMate AI Works

From opening the app to mastering a subject — five simple steps to academic excellence.

01

Open & Sync

Splash screen with auto-login. Dashboard loads instantly with today's XP goal, due flashcards, and streak status.

02

Review Flashcards

FSRS algorithm presents cards due for review. Rate Again/Hard/Good/Easy — the system learns your memory patterns.

03

Focus Session

Start a Pomodoro timer. Study distraction-free. Every minute earns XP that levels you up.

04

Take a Quiz

Subject-wise quizzes with performance tracking. Identify weak areas and improve over time.

05

Analyze & Repeat

Check analytics, claim daily rewards, compete on leaderboards. Every session makes you better.

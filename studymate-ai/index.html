⚡ Process

How StudyMate Works

From opening the app to mastering a subject — a seamless learning flow.

01

Open & Sync

Splash screen with auto-login. Your dashboard loads instantly with today's goals, XP, and due flashcards.

02

Review Flashcards

FSRS algorithm shows cards due for review. Rate Again/Hard/Good/Easy — the system learns your memory pattern.

03

Focus Session

Start a Pomodoro timer. Study distraction-free. Every minute earns XP that levels you up.

04

Take a Quiz

Test your knowledge with subject-wise quizzes. Track accuracy over time and identify weak areas.

05

Analyze & Repeat

Check analytics, claim daily rewards, compete with friends, and repeat. Every session makes you better.
